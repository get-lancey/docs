---
title: 'Gong'
description: 'Find feature requests in your Gong calls and connect them to your product initatives automatically.'
---

### Prerequisites

- Administrator level access to generate API keys within Gong

> **Note:** Lancey will bring in last 30 days call transcripts by default. If you'd like to change this limit, mention this to your Lancey point of contact.


### Gong Instructions
<Steps>
  <Step title="Company Settings">
    Head to your Gong instance (or send this page to your Gong admin) and navigate to **Company** settings.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Ecosystem / API">
    Find the Ecosystem section and click on **API**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="API Keys">
    Click on the **API Keys** tab and then the **Get API Key** button.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Details">
    Make note of the **Base URL**, **Access Key**, and **Secret Key**. You'll need these three items to set up the integration on Lancy side.
  </Step>
</Steps>

Now that you have your API details from Gong, it's time to head over to your [Lancey dashboard](https://app.lancey.ai). 

### Lancey Instructions
<Steps>
  <Step title="Finding Gong integration">
    Use this [link](https://app.lancey.ai/settings/integrations/gong.io/manage) to navigate to the Gong integration inside of Lancey.
  </Step>
  <Step title="API Url">
    Paste in the **Base URL** from Gong into this field. 
  </Step>
  <Step title="Access Key">
    Paste in the **Access Key** from Gong into this field. 
  </Step>
  <Step title="Access Key Secret">
    Paste in the **Secret Key** from Gong into this field. 
  </Step>
  <Step title="Test connection">
    Once you've provided the three required fields, press the **Connect using credentials** button
  </Step>
</Steps>
